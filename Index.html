Past Experiences and Projects





I am currently working in the research department of the UCSD Eating Disorders Center for Treatment and Research. I help by analyzing data, helping in logistics, and/or providing assistance during research procedures. All of this is done within the neurosciences and brain imaging department of the Research center.
I’m building a program for the center that is able to display patient’s test results based on several arithmetic processes, that I’m translating from another coding language into Java, that depend on patients’ answers to several questionnaires regarding their recovering process of eating disorders.

For the summer of 2016, I assisted in the logistics and accounting departments at Comenzar de Nuevo A.C., an organization dedicated to help people with eating disorders. I did so by providing assistance by organizing patients' files, designing a program to store all files, and helping create a more efficient working environment.
At Comenzar de Nuevo I built a program that was able to help doctors keep track of their input and output of patients' medications more effectively. I created a database where doctors could input values, set alarms for when supplies were low, pull information, and overall work more efficiently than before.
